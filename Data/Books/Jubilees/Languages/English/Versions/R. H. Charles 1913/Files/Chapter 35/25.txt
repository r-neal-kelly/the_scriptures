²⁰ And she said unto him: ‘I ask you that the day I die, thou wilt take me in and bury me near Sarah, thy fatherʼs mother, and that thou and Jacob will love each other, and that neither will desire evil against the other, but mutual love only, and (so) ye will prosper, my sons, and be honoured in the midst of the land, and no enemy will rejoice over you, and ye will be a blessing and a mercy in the eyes of all those that love you.’
