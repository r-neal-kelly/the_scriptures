⁷ And he grew up, and dwelt in Ur of the Chaldees, near to the father of his wifeʼs mother, and he worshipped idols, and he took to himself a wife in the thirty‑sixth jubilee, in the fifth week, in the first year thereof, and her name was Mêlkâ, the daughter of Kâbêr, the daughter of his fatherʼs brother.
