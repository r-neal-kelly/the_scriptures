³⁵ And Japheth and his sons went towards the sea and dwelt in the land of their portion, and Madai saw the land of the sea and it did not please him, and he begged a (portion) from Elam and Asshur and Arpachshad, his wifeʼs brother, and he dwelt in the land of Media, near to his wifeʼs brother until this day.
